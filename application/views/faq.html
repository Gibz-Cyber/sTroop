faq

frequently asked questions, get knowledge beforehand

If you wish to advertise your product on SupplyTroopLK, You must register to an account. But you can search & respond to any ad without registering.

To sign up for SupplyTroopLK simply you can click on sign up tab and follow the instruction. Once you have submitted your details for sign up for SupplyTroopLK, we will send you an email to verify your account.

To edit your account details, first, you have to log in to your account & then go to Account page.

If you want to set a new password for your account you have to log in to your account and go to Account page & then you can change your password.

If you are unable to log in to your account, first you have to make sure that you have registered for SupplyTroopLK. If you have registered for SupplyTroopLK, enter correct email address and correct password when signing in. If you still can’t log in to your account, please contact us .

If you can’t remember your account password please click the forget password link and follow the instructions.

Advertising on SupplyTroopLK

To advertise on SupplyTroopLK, the first step is to sign in to your account. Then you can simply click the Post Ad tab & follow the instruction. Advertising on SupplyTroopLK is quick, simple and completely free.

If you wish to delete your ad, you have to sign in to your account and go to active ads page and click delete my ad button.

To edit your ad, log in to your account and go to active ads page and click edit option on your ad.

Your ads appear on SupplyTroopLK for 60 days unless you delete them manually through your profile.

Simply you can find your ad from your account page. Or, it may not appear on our site due to following reasons.

  • If your ad still under review – when you post your ad on SupplyTroopLK it will take less than 02 hours (working hours) to approved by SupplyTroopLK team.
  • Your ad may have been rejected – Your ad could get rejected due to violation of our rules and conditions (if your ad had been rejected, you should have received an e mail from our team .therefore check your emails for such a email.

The rules for posting an ad on SupplyTroopLK are given below. The following types of ads are not allowed on SupplyTroopLK.

  1. Illegal items or services on Sri Lanka.
  2. An item or service not located in Sri Lanka.
  3. An invalid phone number or invalid email address
  4. Offensive language & offensive pictures
  5. An unrealistic offer
  6. Two different title and description (which is not relevant to the description.)
  7. Any advertisement related to animals (such as pets, agricultural animals/animal abuse items
  8. Any foods related with animals (meat, fish, eggs etc.)
  9. A URL link that is not relevant to the advertised item or service offers & request for items and services in the same ad
  10. If images have watermarks (Copy-righted images)

If you haven’t received any response to your ad, you can look at how to sell fast page and try again.
